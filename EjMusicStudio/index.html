About Us

Hello there!

Ej Sound Studios is a penthouse garden studio, a premier audio recording and music production studio based in Bangalore. We handle all your music and audio needs starting from composing, to recording, to music production (programming), to audio dubbing, to sound effects, to voice over's, to mixing and mastering. We also offer video shooting and complete post production services

EJ's obsession to music has resulted in the state-of-the art facilities and pars with all international industry standards. At EJ we do not comprise on quality. We are always on the process of elevating our benchmark. EJ is equipped with the most sophisticated audio and video gadgets, thus generating the best and pure form of sound and music.

Ellwyn Joshua Music Director founder of EJ Sound Studios started his studio in the year 2010. After completing his engineering in the branch of electronics and communications he started working full time in the music industry. As a music director he has worked and completed 3 Feature films and over 50 Short films. He was nominated the "Best Upcoming music director in the Radio Mirchi Music Awards 2012". Baby Box the short film for which he composed origional score has won making a difference award in the "8th Comm fest film festival , Canada", It has also "won the best national short film award" in the International film festival , Gujarat. He has also worked on several TV commercials, Radio jingles, music albums and corporate films.
